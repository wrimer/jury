Our Company

Jury.Online is a platform dedicated to security of investing into ICO projects with an integrated decentralized escrow service that provides our clients with the maximum security due to the absence of third party control over the funds.

In case of controversies between Investor and Project at any stage of its implementation the protocol offers unbiased mediation

How it works

Each project coming to Jury.Online has a roadmap with clear transitional stages. All stages are being kept on an invulnerable smart contract, which is created when investing. When a project completes a milestone - only then it receives the next part of invested funds for its further development.

If the project does not fulfilled its obligations - completed a milestone out of line or with improper quality - the investor has the right to open a dispute. Then within 1 working day Jury.Online mediators will closely consider the problem and make a decision. If it turns out that the project is not right, the investor has the right to return his funds up to 99,5%.
