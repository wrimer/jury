Our Company

Jury.Online is a platform dedicated to security of investing into ICO projects with an integrated decentralized escrow service that provides our clients with the maximum security due to the absence of third party control over the funds. In case of controversies between Investor and Project at any stage of its implementation the protocol offers unbiased mediation

Each project coming to Jury.Online has a roadmap with clear transitional stages. All stages are being kept on an invulnerable smart contract, which is created when investing. When a project completes a milestone - only then it receives the next part of invested funds for its further development.
